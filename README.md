### Global Premium vs Regional Premium

This is the part that confuses a lot of new users, so let's clear it up.

**Global Premium** is for users in 23 developed countries: United States, Canada, Japan, South Korea, Germany, France, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, and others including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and Israel. If you're in one of these countries, PikPak will automatically show you Global Premium pricing at checkout.

Global Premium gives you the highest download speeds — currently up to **20 MB/s** — with no regional restrictions. Even if you travel to other countries, your speeds stay consistent.

**Regional Premium** is for users outside those 23 countries. You get the same 10TB storage, same core features, and the same video streaming quality. The main trade-off is download speed (capped at around **5 MB/s**) and that speed may be restricted if you travel to a Global Premium country. The upside is the price is significantly lower.

PikPak automatically detects your country and shows you the right pricing at checkout, so you don't have to figure out which tier you fall into manually.
